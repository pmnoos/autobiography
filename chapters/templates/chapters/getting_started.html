Creating Your First Chapter

Step 1: Click "New Chapter" in the navigation bar

Step 2: Fill in the chapter details:

  • Title: Give your chapter a meaningful title
  • Subtitle: Add a brief description
  • Content: Write your story using the rich text editor
  • Order: Set the sequence (1, 2, 3, etc.)
  • Slug: This creates the URL (auto-generated from title)

Step 3: Click "Save" to create your chapter

Adding Photos to Your Gallery

Step 1: Click "Upload Images" in the navigation

Step 2: Fill in the image details:

  • Image: Select a photo from your device
  • Title: Give your image a title
  • Description: Add context about the photo
  • Chapter: Link to a specific chapter (optional)
  • Date Taken: When the photo was taken (optional)

Step 3: Click "Upload" to add the image

Managing Your Content

Admin Panel: Access advanced features through the admin panel

  • Edit existing chapters and images
  • Delete content you no longer want
  • Manage user accounts
  • Export your content as backup
